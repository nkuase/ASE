# Course Grading

---

## Total: 1000 Points

- **5 Assignments** – 250 pts  
- **2 Projects** – 350 pts  
- **2 Midterms** – 350 pts  
- **Daily Quizzes** – 50 pts  
- **Bonus** – up to 50 pts

---

## Homework (25%)

- **5 Assignments:** 5 × 50 = 250 pts  
- Deadlines are firm.
- Extension possible if requested ≥ 48 hrs before due date (−20% penalty).

---

## Projects (35%)

- **Team Project** – 200 pts (20%)
  - For the courses with no team project, another individual project is added.
- **Individual Project** – 150 pts (15%)  
- Regular status updates to communicate progress.

---

## Midterms (35%)

- **2 Exams:** 150 + 200 = 350 pts  
- Online via Canvas; 2nd exam is comprehensive.  
- One double-sided cheat sheet allowed.  
- Strict time limits to prevent cheating.

---

## Daily Quizzes (5%)

- Total: 50 pts  
- Held at the start of class to review key ideas.  
- Points awarded for all submissions, even blank.

---

## Bonus Points (Up to 5%)

- **Max:** 50 pts  
- Earned for improving the course:  
  - Providing feedback  
  - Finding/fixing bugs in course materials or programs

---

## Apply the P4M4 Model

1. Projects Are Your Priority

- Add at least one project to your portfolio after every course.

2. Divide and Conquer with Practice

- Progress step-by-step through focused, manageable practices.

---

3. Begin with the Magic, Grow with Principles

- Start without overthinking; discover the principles powering the "magic."

4. Master Many Patterns

- Patterns are your secret weapon for solving real-world problems efficiently.
